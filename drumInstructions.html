Welcome to the Drum Viz App

This app aims to make it easier to understand musical notation for playing the drums, as well as learning pieces at your own pace.

When the app loads you will see a musical score towards the top, some playback controls at the top right and a three-dimensional model of a drumkit in the main window. The playback controls allow the piece to be played at a particular tempo, denoted by the number in the centre of the dial.

This can be altered by either dragging the dial round to the desired location or using the mousewheel to increase/decrease the tempo. If you press the play button then the piece will start and you'll see a red line move across the score denoting where abouts in the bar the piece is currently playing.

You can pause the recording at any time by clicking on the pause button and you may reset the piece back to the beginning by clicking rewind.

As the piece progresses you'll also notice that the particular drum being played is identified on the 3D drumkit by a small, red sphere above the relevant drum.

This is designed to offer further aid in the playback as you can identify which drums are being played at each particular note.

The drop down menu beneath the playback controls denotes which score is being played, the full title of which appears above the score whenever you select a different piece. In the default setting there are three scores that may be played, each with a subtle variation of the others.

Further pieces will be added in time, along with a number of updates and features.
